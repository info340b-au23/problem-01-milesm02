Luffy

Luffy is the main protagonist of One Piece, a manga series written by Eiichiro Oda that began running in 1997, and still runs to this day. Luffy is an airheaded but competent pirate captain with goals to conquer the Grand Line, acquire the One Piece, and become the King of the Pirates!

  • I admire Luffy for his courage and willingness to die for his dreams
  • I admire Luffy for his ability to almost always be happy, regardless of fate or fortune.
  • I admire Luffy for his commitment to friends and family, and his unwillingness to leave someone behind
